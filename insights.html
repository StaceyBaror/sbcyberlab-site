Secure SDLC: what “good” looks like

A simple blueprint that helps teams improve security posture without slowing delivery.

  • Define security gates that fit your CI/CD reality.
  • Make threat modeling lightweight but repeatable.
  • Use measurable controls (not “security theater”).

Forensic readiness in SaaS systems

Investigations fail when evidence is missing or unreliable. Start with minimum viable readiness.

  • Log the right events with integrity and timestamps.
  • Define retention and access controls for evidence.
  • Document investigation workflows before incidents happen.

Prefer a readiness assessment sprint? Request a consultation.

Insight

DFIR playbooks that actually work

Playbooks succeed when they are trigger-based, owned, and actionable under pressure.

  • Define triggers, owners, and escalation criteria.
  • Separate “containment” from “investigation.”
  • Practice with tabletop exercises and iterate.
